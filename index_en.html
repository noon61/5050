01
First-time fun in brushing your teeth by yourself

It promotes “아이와같이” (Aiwa Katchi) as a fun way to help children transition from brushing with parents to brushing on their own. Through playful learning of correct techniques, children gain confidence, and daily brushing becomes enjoyable. It also encourages fostering independence and strengthening parent-child bonds.

02
Let's learn Hangul in a fun way!

It’s a Hangul “이”-shaped product that helps Korean children new to reading naturally get familiar with letters while brushing. It sparks interest in Hangul, connects letter learning with daily toothbrushing, and supports both language development and overall growth.
