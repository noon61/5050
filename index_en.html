01
Enjoy brushing your child's teeth

This promotes “아이와같이” (Aiwa Katchi) as a fun way to help children transition from brushing with parents to brushing on their own. Through playful learning of correct techniques, children gain confidence, and daily brushing becomes enjoyable. It also encourages independence and strengthens parent-child bonds.

02
With ‘이’: Independence and Healthy Teeth

This product, based on the Hangul letter “이” (ee), helps children beginning to read and write naturally learn letters while brushing their teeth. The shape resembles the mouth when pronouncing “ee” and matches toothbrushing movements, making learning Hangul fun in daily life.
